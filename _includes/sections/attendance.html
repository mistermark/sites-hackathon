The Ones to Beat

Last year, 15 teams competed to hack the financial system. In the end, team Exodus took the prize with their 3D-printed piggy bank that counted the actual coins you were saving up. This information was registered and sent to the online banking platform, which displayed a real-time balance to help children learn the benefits of saving up their pennies.
